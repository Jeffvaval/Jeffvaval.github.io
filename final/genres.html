Anime Genres

Each genre gives anime a different style, mood, and type of story.

Action

Action anime focuses on battles, adventure, danger, and exciting storylines.

Romance

Romance anime focuses on relationships, emotions, and character growth.

Horror

Horror anime includes fear, mystery, monsters, dark themes, and suspense.

Comedy

Comedy anime uses humor, jokes, and fun characters to entertain viewers.
