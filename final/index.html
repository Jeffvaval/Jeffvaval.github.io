Popular Anime

Action

Demon Slayer

Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps after his family is killed, fighting demons while seeking a cure for his sister Nezuko.

Supernatural

Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori battles deadly curses with trained sorcerers, combining fast-paced action with supernatural threats.

Dark

Attack on Titan

Humanity hides behind walls to survive Titans, as secrets and war shape a dark, high-stakes story.

Hero

My Hero Academia

In a world of superpowers, Izuku trains at U.A. to become a hero, focusing on growth, teamwork, and courage.

Ninja

Naruto

A young ninja with a sealed beast strives to earn respect and become Hokage through hard work and friendship.

Mystery

Death Note

Light Yagami uses a lethal notebook to judge criminals, leading to a tense battle of wits with detective L.
