About Anime World

A beginner-friendly guide to anime

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce beginners to anime in a simple and easy way. It helps users understand different genres and discover popular shows.

Audience

This website is designed for people who are new to anime or want quick recommendations. It is also useful for fans looking to explore new genres.

Goal

The goal of this website is to help users find anime they enjoy, learn about different types of anime, and have a fun experience exploring content.
